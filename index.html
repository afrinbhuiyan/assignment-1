The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus is a visionary Bangladeshi social entrepreneur, economist, and the founder of the Grameen Bank. He pioneered the concept of microcredit and microfinance, empowering millions of people, especially women, to break the cycle of poverty through small loans and sustainable entrepreneurship. His innovative approach has transformed lives worldwide and earned him the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006. Dr. Yunus continues to inspire global efforts for social and economic development.
